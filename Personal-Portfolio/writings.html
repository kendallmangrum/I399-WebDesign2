Writing as a Release

For a few years now I have found myself writing short pieces and poems as a form of release and a creative outlet. I've never been the type of person to really share what I'm thinking so writing things down on paper serves as a way to get things out of my mind.

Being an a quiet overthinking, my writings tend to be on the sad, depressing side but I think they carry some weight. Depending on the person reading them, they can hit home pretty hard. I hope you enjoy them.

Poetry

Alone

Head full of fears
Eyes wet with tears
Wishing someone would stay near

No one gets the things the way I do
Yeah I have friends but I'm a loner too
Isolated by myself like I'm part of a zoo.

I just want someone to love and to hold
Having someone for that would be worth more than gold
But here I am, all alone and cold.

My World

In her eyes the galaxies were kept. Whenever I looked into them I saw the twinkling of countless stars. I often got lost in her eyes; their beauty so alluring.

Her hair could be seen styled as the waves of the ocean, Flowing gently from her head to just below the shoulders.

Her smile glowed like the moon; Even in the darkest of rooms its light still shined bright.

Her soul was as free as the wind; Nothing could contain her. Her presence, or lack thereof, could always be felt.

Her personality radiated from her like the sun. She had a warmth about her that could comfort any anxiety.

She meant the world to me. When I held her in my arms I had everything I needed. But just as the tide comes and goes, So did she and now I'm left without a world to call my own.

Eternal Love

You not loving me is a hard pill to swallow.
I thought maybe you'd change your mind, but you didn't and now I feel so hollow.

I don't think I've ever loved someone as much as I love you.
It doesn't matter though, it just got my heart torn in two.

Turns out that you're just my type,
And I was down to be in a relationship feeling so hype.

No one had ever made me feel the way you did.
Deeper and deeper in love I slid.

In my mind I kept thinking you were the one,
But before I knew it the idea of “us” was done.

But you're still figuring things out.
A relationship with me at the time had you filled with doubt.

I know I still have flaws but that's okay.
I'll eventually be with someone, today's just not that day.

And who knows, maybe someday everything will come full circle,
And we'll end up together cause my love for you is eternal.

Today's World

Today's world is so messed up
Filled with guys sliding into DMs just saying sup
People pretending to care
Saying that they'll always be there
But as soon as they find someone new
They forget all about you
It's starting to be hard finding someone who's true to their word
Finding someone who truly wants the best for you, that's kinda unheard
It seems like in today's world filled with Snapchat streaks
That that's when people think their friendship is at its peak
People don't really care about each other anymore
As soon as they get the chance they walk right out the door
Being surrounded by people who want things that are temporary
Makes me feel like someone who isn't considered ordinary
No, I want to love someone with all my heart and soul
For them to complete me and make me feel whole
I know I'm not perfect but I'll give them my all
I will always stand beside them and not let them fall
I want us to be an example for all to see
That our love for each other is how it's supposed to be
